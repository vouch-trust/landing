Cryptography, abstracted.

Vouch uses standard Ed25519 signatures and DID:Web infrastructure, hidden behind a zero-friction browser extension.

1. Sign & Send

Agents click one button in Gmail or Zendesk. The extension hashes the message and applies a cryptographic signature using a locally stored private key.

2. Passive Verification

The receiver's extension scans the DOM, reads the signature, and instantly queries the decentralized trust registry to verify the sender's public key.

3. The Kill Switch

When an employee leaves, IT revokes their access. Within milliseconds, all their past and future intercepted signatures turn red globally.
